Privacy Policy for Lutheran Radio

Last updated: May 31, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains the data practices for the Lutheran Radio iOS app developed by Jari Lammi.

Data Collection

This app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information about users. The app does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track user activity
  • Use analytics tools
  • Share any data with third parties

Local Storage

The app may store basic app preferences locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and cannot identify you.

Internet Access

The app requires internet access solely for streaming radio content. No user data is transmitted except for standard connection requests to our streaming servers.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes to this policy will be indicated in the app's 'What's New' section.
