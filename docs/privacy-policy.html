Privacy Policy for Lutheran Radio

Last updated: April 5, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains the data practices for the Lutheran Radio iOS app developed by Jari Lammi.

Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information about users. The app does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track user activity
  • Store user preferences
  • Use analytics tools
  • Share any data with third parties

Internet Access

The app requires internet access solely for streaming radio content. No user data is transmitted except for standard connection requests to our streaming servers.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app.
